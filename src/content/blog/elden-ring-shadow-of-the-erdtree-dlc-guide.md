## Introduction: The Tarnished’s New Calling

The Lands Between are vast, but the ambition of the Tarnished is vaster. Since its 2022 debut, *Elden Ring* has redefined the action RPG landscape, offering unparalleled freedom, challenge, and dense lore. Now, after years of anticipation, FromSoftware has delivered its monumental single story expansion: **Shadow of the Erdtree (SotE)**.

This isn't merely an update; it is an epic new chapter. **Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree** takes players far beyond the familiar, plunging us into the **Land of Shadow**—a realm steeped in hidden history, ruled by the shadowy figure of Messmer the Impaler, and haunted by the legacy of the Empyrean Miquella.

For veterans and new players alike, navigating this colossal **Elden Ring DLC** requires preparation, knowledge, and perhaps a little bit of strategic planning. This definitive guide will serve as your beacon, covering everything from the precise **SotE release date** and essential entry requirements to the deepest **Miquella lore**, the terrifying new bosses, and the revolutionary **Elden Ring DLC weapons** you'll uncover.

Whether you're struggling to locate the **Mohgwyn Palace DLC entrance** or optimizing your build for the grueling new challenge, we have the grounded, fact-checked information you need to walk the path of Miquella and confront the secrets hidden beneath the blackened boughs of the Erdtree.

## The Essentials: Release Date, Price, and Editions

Before strapping on your Greatsword, the fundamental logistical questions must be answered. When can you play, and how much will it cost to step into the **Land of Shadow**?

### SotE Release Date and Availability

The official **SotE release date** was June 21, 2024. The expansion launched simultaneously worldwide across all supported platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Xbox Series X|S, Xbox One, and PC (via Steam). Given the scale of this **Elden Ring story expansion**, many players dedicated full weeks just to scratching the surface of the new content.

### Erdtree DLC Price and Editions

The **Erdtree DLC price** is set at a premium reflecting its sheer size—a standard for major FromSoftware expansions. Typically, the expansion is priced around $39.99 USD / £34.99 GBP.

For dedicated fans and collectors, the **Shadow of the Erdtree collectors edition** offered various premium physical goods, often including a statue of Messmer the Impaler, an art book, and a digital soundtrack. Given the massive nature of this content drop, purchasing the base game bundled with the DLC, known as the *Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree Edition*, became a popular option for newcomers.

The **Elden Ring DLC download size** is substantial, comparable to a full AAA title patch, often exceeding 15 GB, making preload essential for launch day players. Ensure you have the latest **Elden Ring update** installed before attempting to download the expansion.

## Mandatory Prerequisites: How to Start Elden Ring DLC

This is the most crucial section for any Tarnished eager to dive in. Unlike some previous FromSoftware DLCs that offered immediate access, *Shadow of the Erdtree* mandates that players meet two very specific, high-level requirements. Without completing both steps, the entry point will remain inert.

### Step 1: Defeat Starscourge Radahn

The first mandatory boss is **Starscourge Radahn**, located in Caelid. This is a key checkpoint required for the progression of the base game, but it serves a dual purpose here: allowing access to the next requirement.

### Step 2: Defeat Mohg, Lord of Blood

The final, non-negotiable requirement is the defeat of **Mohg, Lord of Blood**. Mohg guards the entrance to the Land of Shadow.

#### Locating the Mohgwyn Palace DLC Entrance

To reach Mohg, players must travel to the hidden region of **Mohgwyn Palace**. There are two primary ways to get there:

1.  **Late-Game Teleportation:** Use the Sending Gate found in the Consecrated Snowfield. This requires acquiring both halves of the Haligtree Secret Medallion.
2.  **Early-Game Access (The White Mask Varre Questline):** This is the more common and faster method.
    *   Find White Mask Varre at the first site of grace in Limgrave, then after Godrick’s defeat, he moves to the Rose Church in Liurnia.
    *   Complete his tasks, which involve invading other players (even if you fail the invasions) and using the **Pureblood Knight’s Medal**.
    *   Using the medal teleports you directly to Mohgwyn Palace.

Once Mohg, Lord of Blood, is defeated, the **Mohgwyn Palace DLC entrance** opens. You must interact with the arm of the Empyrean Miquella at the site of grace in Mohg's boss room.

> *Pro Tip: Many players rush to Mohg just for the DLC. Be warned, Mohg is an extremely tough optional boss, even for high-level characters. Mastering his bloodflame attacks and the Nihil mechanic is paramount.*

## Preparing for the Land of Shadow: Level and Builds

The Lands Between prepared you, but the **Land of Shadow** demands specialization. FromSoftware has stated that the difficulty of the **Elden Ring story expansion** is scaled to challenge players who have completed most of the base game's optional content.

### What Level for Shadow of the Erdtree?

While there is no fixed minimum level coded into the game, based on the difficulty spike observed in the DLC, the consensus is:

*   **Recommended Minimum Level:** 120–150
*   **Optimal Level:** 150+ (Especially for NG+ cycles)

The developers implemented a unique **Elden Ring gameplay change** with the introduction of *Scadutree Fragments* and *Revered Spirit Ashes*. These items grant a DLC-specific scaling boost to your damage output and damage resistance, as well as strengthening your Spirit Ashes, respectively. This system effectively soft-resets the power curve, meaning even over-leveled characters (Level 200+) cannot simply steamroll the early DLC bosses.

## The Narrative Core: Miquella, Messmer, and the Land of Shadow Lore

The **Elden Ring story expansion** is fundamentally built around the fate of Miquella, the twin brother of Malenia, and the journey he undertook into the mysterious region known as the **Land of Shadow**.

### Miquella Lore: The Unalloyed

Miquella the Kind, an Empyrean cursed with eternal childhood, sought to cure his sister Malenia of her Scarlet Rot. His ultimate plan was to create an alternative to the Golden Order—the Unalloyed Gold. This pursuit led him to slumber within the cocoon we find in Mohgwyn Palace.

The **Shadow of the Erdtree** narrative follows Miquella's physical path, guided by the remnants of his followers. The player is literally "walking in Miquella's footsteps," exploring the land where the Golden Order first began to crumble. The **Land of Shadow** is where Queen Marika first arrived and where the primary history of the Lands Between was erased or, as some **Shadow of the Erdtree lore theories** suggest, *sealed*. The very Erdtree of this land has been scorched black, symbolizing a failed or forgotten beginning.

### Messmer the Impaler: The New Antagonist

Every major FromSoftware title needs an iconic villain, and **Messmer the Impaler** steps into this role with chilling authority. Revealed as potentially another child of Marika, Messmer is shown wielding immense, serpent-like fire and acting as the jailer or ruler of the Land of Shadow.

His visual design—mixing royal armor with burning motifs and a terrifying visage—suggests a kinship with the other demigods, yet one who has been entirely excluded from the major historical texts. His central role is hinted at through promotional materials, suggesting he may be the one who "burned" the original Land of Shadow, perhaps at the behest of Marika or some even older entity.

Messmer is likely one of the most challenging **new Elden Ring bosses**, requiring players to master dodge timings and position in tight arenas. Defeating him promises huge lore revelations about Marika’s family and the establishment of the Golden Order itself.

### The Role of the Land of Shadow

The **Land of Shadow** is presented as a mirror to the Lands Between, a place overshadowed by the main Erdtree, where the very concept of grace was first established and then corrupted or abandoned. The map size is staggering, estimated to be roughly equivalent to Limgrave, Liurnia, and Caelid combined, offering dozens of new dungeons, legacy areas, and secrets. It is a fully realized, new world, reinforcing that this is a substantial **Elden Ring story expansion**, not just a small add-on.

## New Content Deep Dive: Weapons, Ashes, and Gameplay Changes

FromSoftware always uses DLC to expand the game’s combat sandbox, and *Shadow of the Erdtree* is no exception. It introduces a massive influx of **FromSoftware new content**, ensuring that even players with hundreds of hours will have completely new ways to approach combat.

### Elden Ring DLC Weapons and Armor

The most exciting addition is not just new weapons, but **new weapon types**. SotE introduces eight new core weapon categories, fundamentally changing the metagame:

1.  **Reverse-Grip Katanas/Light Swords:** Focus on quick, consecutive slashes and high-mobility attacks.
2.  **Great Katanas:** Larger, slower katanas that bridge the gap between normal katanas and colossal weapons, often featuring unique Ash of War abilities.
3.  **Throwing Weapons (e.g., Throwing Daggers):** A dedicated slot for weapons that can be thrown and retrieved, integrating a new ranged option for melee builds.
4.  **Hand-to-Hand Arts:** Think martial arts. Fast, close-range combat focused on punching and kicking, often using Dexterity or Strength/Faith scaling.

This massive infusion of **Elden Ring DLC weapons** allows for deep re-speccing and experimentation. It is recommended that players explore the Land of Shadow thoroughly to find the early examples of these new classes, as they can significantly impact your progression through the DLC.

### New Spirit Ashes and Combat Dynamics

In addition to the Scadutree Fragment scaling for existing Spirit Ashes, the DLC introduces **new spirit ashes Elden Ring** players can acquire, many tied to the new enemies encountered in the Land of Shadow. These new summons are essential for drawing boss aggro and dealing auxiliary damage, given the high health pools of the **new Elden Ring bosses**.

The core **Elden Ring gameplay changes** in the DLC focus heavily on verticality and stealth. The **Erdtree map size** is not only broad but incredibly tall, encouraging exploration of cliffsides, ruins, and hidden paths that often yield Scadutree Fragments.

## Technical Deep Dive: Performance, Patches, and Download

Given the massive scale of the content, technical stability is paramount for a smooth launch experience.

### Elden Ring Update and Performance Patch

The launch of *Shadow of the Erdtree* coincided with a major **Elden Ring update** (Patch 1.12 or similar), which laid the foundation for the DLC content, including the necessary game balance adjustments and user interface elements for the Scadutree Fragment mechanic.

A key focus of this accompanying update was the **Elden Ring performance patch**. While the base game already ran well, the DLC's graphical intensity and density of effects (especially fire and massive world pieces) required optimization. Players on PC and console saw improvements in shader compilation and frame rate stability, though the demanding nature of the new environments still pushed hardware limits.

### How Long is Shadow of the Erdtree?

For those wondering **how long is Shadow of the Erdtree**, the answer varies significantly based on playstyle and experience, but general estimates place it among the longest single DLCs in recent history:

*   **Main Storyline (Speedrun/Minimalist):** 25–35 hours
*   **Completionist (All Dungeons, Scadutree Fragments, and Optional Bosses):** 60–80+ hours

The sheer **Erdtree map size** and the complexity of the hidden dungeons contribute to this extraordinary length, making the **Erdtree DLC price** feel entirely justified by the sheer volume of content.

The scope of this DLC ensures *Elden Ring* remains a relevant and evolving masterwork for years to come.

## FAQs (People Also Ask)

### Q1. Is Shadow of the Erdtree included in the Elden Ring base game?

No, **Shadow of the Erdtree** is a separate, paid downloadable content (**Elden Ring DLC**) expansion. It must be purchased separately from the base game, although it is often sold bundled with the base game in the *Shadow of the Erdtree Edition*.

### Q2. How do I access the Land of Shadow in Elden Ring?

To access the Land of Shadow, players must defeat two mandatory base game bosses: **Starscourge Radahn** (in Caelid) and **Mohg, Lord of Blood** (in Mohgwyn Palace). Once both are defeated, you interact with Miquella’s cocoon in Mohg’s boss arena (the **Mohgwyn Palace DLC entrance**).

### Q3. What is the recommended level for Shadow of the Erdtree?

Most players find the challenging difficulty of the **Elden Ring Shadow of the Erdtree** content is best approached around **Level 120–150**. The DLC introduces a unique scaling system using *Scadutree Fragments* that limits over-leveling, so preparation and specific build optimization are more important than excessive level grinding.

### Q4. Who is Messmer the Impaler, and what is his role in the DLC?

**Messmer the Impaler** is the central antagonist and one of the **new Elden Ring bosses** in the DLC. He is strongly implied to be another child of Queen Marika, perhaps one exiled or forgotten. He is the ruler of the **Land of Shadow** and a key figure in the historical events surrounding Miquella’s journey.

### Q5. Are there new weapons and armor types in the Shadow of the Erdtree DLC?

Yes, the **Elden Ring DLC weapons** include several entirely new weapon categories, such as Reverse-Grip Katanas, Great Katanas, and dedicated Hand-to-Hand martial arts weapons. This provides significant **Elden Ring gameplay changes** and build versatility.

### Q6. What is the approximate download size for the Elden Ring DLC?

The **Elden Ring DLC download size** is substantial, generally ranging from 15 GB to 20 GB, depending on the platform. Players should ensure they have sufficient space and download the latest accompanying **Elden Ring update** for stability.

### Q7. Does the DLC add an Elden Ring final boss DLC?

Yes. While **Messmer the Impaler** is a critical boss, the **Elden Ring story expansion** culminates in a distinct, extremely challenging final encounter that ties into the overarching narrative of Miquella and Marika's past, effectively serving as a true **Elden Ring final boss DLC**.

### Q8. What are Scadutree Fragments?

Scadutree Fragments are a new collectible item introduced specifically for the **Shadow of the Erdtree** content. Collecting and using these fragments at Sites of Grace permanently increases your character's damage output and damage resistance *only within the Land of Shadow*, acting as a necessary mechanic to tackle the DLC's difficulty.
