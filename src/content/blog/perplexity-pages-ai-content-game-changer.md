## How to Use Perplexity Pages: A Step-by-Step Tutorial

The process for creating a Page is remarkably straightforward, emphasizing **easy content creation** over complex prompt engineering. Access to Pages is generally part of the **Perplexity Pro features** subscription, which unlocks its full potential.

### Step 1: Initiating a New Page

You can start a new Page in one of two ways:

1.  **From a Search Thread:** If you’ve already conducted a detailed search on a topic (e.g., “The economic impact of 6G adoption”), you can hit the ‘Create Page’ button within the results pane. This leverages all the existing research context.
2.  **From the Dashboard:** Select the ‘Pages’ tab and choose ‘New Page.’ Here, you provide a clear, focused title or topic you want the AI to write about.

### Step 2: Defining the Scope and Structure

This is the most critical part of the **Perplexity Pages tutorial**. After entering your core topic, the system asks you to define the audience and structure:

*   **Audience:** Who is this content for (e.g., "Industry Experts," "Beginners," "Students")? This tailors the complexity and tone of the writing.
*   **Structure:** You can choose from predefined layouts (e.g., Tutorial, Report, Article, Presentation) or provide custom subheadings you want the AI to cover. This ensures the output satisfies the exact informational needs of your project. For instance, if you are generating a **Perplexity Pages for students** report, you might specify sections for "Hypothesis," "Methodology," and "Conclusion."
*   **Image Style:** Perplexity Pages can automatically suggest and integrate relevant images generated by AI (usually through specialized integrations like DALL-E or equivalent models), giving the document a professional, visual flair.

### Step 3: Generating and Reviewing the Draft

Once the parameters are set, Perplexity instantly begins generating the Page. This is where the magic of the **AI-generated articles** happens. The AI performs a focused, deep search and synthesizes the findings into the pre-defined structure.

*   The output is a complete article with a title, introduction, structured body, and conclusion.
*   All claims are instantly verifiable via clickable source citations embedded directly within the text blocks.

### Step 4: Customization and Editing

The resulting Page is fully editable. You can adjust the layout, rewrite sections, add new media, and reorder blocks. This feature is key to transforming an **AI-powered report** into a truly personalized, human-reviewed final product.

*   You can delete unnecessary sections, expand on weak areas, or ask the AI to **customize AI reports** further by prompting it to elaborate on a specific paragraph.
*   This human-in-the-loop editing process ensures the content is accurate and aligns with your brand voice or academic requirements.

### Step 5: Publishing and Sharing

When you are ready, the Page can be published instantly via a unique URL, making it highly **shareable AI content**.

*   Pages live on the Perplexity domain (similar to a public Notion page or a Medium article), requiring no external hosting.
*   The clean, minimal design ensures focus on readability, which is crucial for modern digital audiences.
