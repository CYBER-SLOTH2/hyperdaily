## FAQs

### Q1. When do the Paris 2024 Olympics start?

The **Paris 2024 dates** officially begin with preliminary events on Wednesday, July 24, 2024, but the spectacular **Olympics opening ceremony 2024** will take place on Friday, July 26, 2024, marking the official start of the **Summer Games 2024**. The Games conclude on August 11, 2024.

### Q2. What new sport is being added to the Paris 2024 Olympics?

The main **Paris 2024 new sports** addition is **Breaking** (often called breakdancing). This highly acrobatic and artistic sport, which features B-Boys and B-Girls competing in one-on-one battles, will be hosted at the **Place de la Concorde Olympics** urban cluster.

### Q3. Where will the Paris 2024 Opening Ceremony take place?

The **Olympics opening ceremony 2024** will not be held in a traditional stadium. Instead, it will take place along the River Seine, with athletes parading on boats down the river for 6 kilometers, ending the procession at the Trocadéro.

### Q4. How can I watch the Paris Olympics live stream in the US?

For viewers in the US, the primary platform for the **Olympics live stream** and complete **Olympics 2024 schedule** coverage is Peacock, the streaming service owned by NBC Universal, which holds exclusive rights to the Games and will offer comprehensive coverage beyond the network broadcasts.

### Q5. Can I still buy Paris Olympics tickets?

While the initial public sales phases for **Paris Olympics tickets** have concluded, fans can still look for tickets through the official Paris 2024 resale platform (usually opening closer to the event) or by purchasing authorized hospitality packages. Beware of unofficial third-party resellers.

### Q6. What is the Eiffel Tower Stadium?

The **Eiffel Tower Stadium** is the colloquial name for the temporary outdoor venue constructed on the Champ de Mars, directly beneath the Eiffel Tower. It is one of the most picturesque **Paris Olympics venues** and will primarily host the Beach Volleyball competitions.

### Q7. What are the key dates for the major events like Gymnastics and Track and Field?

The **2024 Olympics gymnastics** and **swimming Olympics 2024** finals dominate the first week (late July), while the **track and field Olympics 2024** events, including the prestigious sprints at the **Stade de France events**, are concentrated during the second week (early August).
